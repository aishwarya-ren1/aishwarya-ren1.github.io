About

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science (CS/IT) with a strong pull toward artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, and computer vision. I like taking a real-world problem and working it all the way through — cleaning the data, engineering features, training and evaluating a model, then wrapping it in an interface someone could actually use.

My toolkit centers on Python: NumPy, Pandas, and Scikit-learn for the modeling side, OpenCV for vision work, TensorFlow/Keras for deep learning, and Streamlit for turning a trained model into something interactive. Every project I ship teaches me a little more about model evaluation, deployment, and the gap between "it works in a notebook" and "it works for someone else."

Right now I'm looking for an AI/ML internship where I can work alongside experienced engineers on problems that matter, and keep growing into the machine learning engineer I'm aiming to become.
